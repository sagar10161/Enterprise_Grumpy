I am a family member at the Rochester Insisute of Technology, Golisano College of Computing and Information Technology, School of Informatics, Department of Networking Security and System Administration.

I am particularly interested in Cloud Computing and Virtualization. Because of my interest I frequently find myself in need of a default web iste to deploy on a web server just to show that it works.
